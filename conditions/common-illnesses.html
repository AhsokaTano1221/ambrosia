Common Illnesses

Give users a clearer guide to familiar conditions like colds, flu, and chickenpox without confusing them with urgent disease.

Identification Tips

Fever, cough, congestion, sore throat, rashes, and fatigue are common, but severity and duration still matter.

Symptoms

Cough, fever, sore throat, congestion, rash, tiredness, low appetite.

Prevention

Vaccines, rest, handwashing, hydration, and staying home when sick.

When to act

See a clinician when symptoms are severe, last too long, or include breathing problems.

What to know

Common illnesses can still become serious in young children, older adults, or people with chronic illness.
