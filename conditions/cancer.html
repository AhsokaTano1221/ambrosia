Cancer

Highlight persistent warning signs, screening awareness, and why early evaluation matters.

Identification Tips

Watch for unexplained lumps, bleeding, weight loss, persistent pain, or long-term fatigue without a clear cause.

Symptoms

Symptoms vary, but unusual lumps, weight loss, pain, and persistent changes deserve review.

Prevention

Screening, avoiding tobacco, sun protection, balanced diet, and earlier checkups.

When to act

Get medical advice for symptoms that are unusual, persistent, or progressively worsening.

What to know

Cancer is not one illness, so this page can branch into organ-specific guides and screening schedules.
