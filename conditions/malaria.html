Malaria

Explore fever-based warning signs, travel exposure questions, and mosquito prevention strategies.

Identification Tips

Fever, chills, headache, and nausea after mosquito exposure can require urgent regional context.

Symptoms

Fever, chills, headache, body aches, nausea, vomiting, weakness.

Prevention

Mosquito nets, repellent, screened sleeping areas, and rapid treatment access.

When to act

Seek care urgently for fever after travel or mosquito-heavy exposure in malaria-risk regions.

What to know

Location matters a lot. Regional outbreaks should affect how users interpret fever and chills.
