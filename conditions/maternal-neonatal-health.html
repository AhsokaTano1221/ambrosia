Maternal And Neonatal Health

Bring together pregnancy warning signs, prenatal support, newborn safety, and urgent-care escalation guidance.

Identification Tips

Bleeding, fever, swelling, severe pain, and newborn feeding or breathing problems all deserve quick attention.

Symptoms

Bleeding, fever, severe pain, reduced movement, newborn distress, feeding trouble.

Prevention

Prenatal care, skilled birth support, postnatal visits, and maternal nutrition.

When to act

Get urgent help for heavy bleeding, seizures, severe headache, or newborn breathing issues.
