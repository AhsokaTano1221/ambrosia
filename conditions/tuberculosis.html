Condition Detail

Tuberculosis

Understand persistent cough warning signs, respiratory spread, and when a longer illness needs formal screening.

Identification Tips

Persistent cough, fever, night sweats, chest discomfort, and weight loss deserve timely evaluation.

Symptoms

Cough lasting weeks, night sweats, fever, chest pain, weight loss, fatigue.

Prevention

Early testing, ventilation, treatment adherence, and community screening support.

When to act

Seek care soon if cough lasts more than a few days or includes blood, fever, or major fatigue.

What to know

TB can be treatable, but delayed care increases spread and risk, especially in crowded settings.
