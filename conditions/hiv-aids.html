HIV/AIDS

Learn how HIV affects the body, when to seek testing, and what prevention and long-term support can look like.

Identification Tips

Look for persistent fever, weight loss, recurrent infection, and fatigue, especially with higher-risk exposure history.

Symptoms

Fever, fatigue, night sweats, recurrent infections, swollen lymph nodes, weight loss.

Prevention

Testing, condoms, clean needle access, public-health education, and timely care.

When to act

Seek medical evaluation after possible exposure or when symptoms persist without a clear cause.

What to know

HIV can often be managed well with long-term treatment, so early testing and stigma-free support matter.
