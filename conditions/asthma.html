Condition Detail

Asthma

Support users with trigger awareness, breathing-risk education, and guidance for when symptoms become urgent.

Identification Tips

Wheezing, coughing, chest tightness, and shortness of breath can worsen with smoke, dust, pollen, or illness.

Symptoms

Wheezing, coughing, chest tightness, breathlessness, nighttime flare-ups.

Prevention

Trigger management, inhaler access, air-quality awareness, and regular care.

When to act

Treat worsening breathing trouble or trouble speaking in full sentences as urgent.

What to know

Environmental conditions like wildfire smoke or urban air pollution can change asthma risk quickly.
