Condition Detail

Cardiovascular Disease

Focus on emergency signs, long-term risk reduction, and everyday habits that protect heart health.

Identification Tips

Chest pain, sudden shortness of breath, dizziness, or pressure spreading to the arm or jaw need urgent attention.

Symptoms

Chest discomfort, fatigue, shortness of breath, dizziness, swelling, palpitations.

Prevention

Exercise, balanced eating, blood pressure care, less tobacco exposure, regular screening.

When to act

Treat severe or sudden chest pain as an emergency and seek help immediately.

What to know

Heart disease often develops over time, so education and screening matter before symptoms become severe.
